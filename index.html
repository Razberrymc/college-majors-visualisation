ipsum: Understanding You

By Team UF AIS

Welcome to ipsum. ipsum is a personalized lifestyle tracker to help you live your best life.

ipsum is a web app that creates personalized trend reports based on the data you provide about your day-to-day life. How? The process is simple: you fill out a customized form every day, and at the end of the month (or year) we transform your data into easy-to-understand visualizations. It compares your day-to-day lifestyle trends to unlock your full potential. Start scrolling to get started!

We're taking "understanding yourself" to the next level- with data analytics. With a single scroll, you can answer questions about your day and ipsum will take your data and transform it into patterns to help you.

Your Days at a Glance

Cyan signifies days with a confidence level of 5.

Yellow signifies days with a confidence level of 4.

Light red signifies days with a confidence level of 3.

Magenta signifies days with a confidence level of 2.

Pink signifies days with a confidence level of 1.
